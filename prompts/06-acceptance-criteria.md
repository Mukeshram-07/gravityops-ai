# 06-acceptance-criteria.md

## Product acceptance
- The app launches successfully without broken routes.
- The dashboard displays seeded incidents immediately.
- Incident filters work.
- Incident detail pages load correctly.
- Upload flow accepts sample CSV or JSON.
- Analytics render with believable values.
- Theme toggle works.
- Mobile layout is usable.

## Engineering acceptance
- Backend has modular structure.
- Frontend has reusable components.
- Core routes are tested.
- Seed data script works.
- Build/lint process completes successfully.

## UX acceptance
- No generic template look
- No major spacing or alignment issues
- No empty pages unless intentionally designed as empty state
- Errors are user-friendly
- Loading states are present
- Important badges and statuses are visually distinct

## Portfolio acceptance
- The app looks like a real B2B internal product
- README can explain the product in one paragraph
- Screenshots are demo-worthy
- Architecture is strong enough to discuss in interviews
